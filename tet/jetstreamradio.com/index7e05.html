About
JetStream is a community-powered online radio station. This means we take reviews and input from the community very seriously.

Reviews
Via the Review Form, you can submit feedback for any JetStream Radio content (DJ's, live shows, on-demand content, or an "other" category for things like social media, articles, etc.). This feedback goes to our administration team and allows them to improve and decide on future content.

The Review Form is available at https://jetstreamradio.com/?content=review.

AutoDJ Song Request Form
AutoDJ exists to fill off-times with enjoyable content. Our goal is to maintain a list of active, popular, upbeat songs.

We have added a form to allow our listeners to both request a song's addition or removal to the AutoDJ list. If a song is explicit, doesn't fit the radio's style, or is unpleasant, it can be reported via the AutoDJ Song Request form. If a new song comes out, it can be submitted so it gets added to the list.

The AutoDJ Song Request Form is available at https://jetstreamradio.com/?content=autodjsongform.
